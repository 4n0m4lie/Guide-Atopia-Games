\## 🎯 Objective



\*\*2nd Run\*\* : You can defeat the \*\*(third) boss\*\*.



\---



\## 📋 Steps to Follow



\### 🔹 Initial Preparation

1\. The big button that was gray at the start of your \*\*1st Run\*\* is now white.

2\. \*\*Equip the boss\*\* you just defeated here for a \*\*+5% Atk\*\* bonus.



\---



\### 🔹 Combat Phase

3\. \*\*2 times\*\* \*Area Lv 100\*

4\. \*\*1 time\*\* \*Area Lv 222\*

5\. \*\*3 times\*\* \*Area Lv 350\*

6\. \*\*4 times\*\* \*Area Lv 500\*

7\. \*\*3 times\*\* \*Area Lv 750\*

8\. \*\*Defeat the first boss\*\* : \*Leader of Looters\*

&#x20;  - (⚡ \*\*19 Energy remaining\*\*)



\---

\### 🔹 Equipment

9\. Buy and equip the \*\*Axe\*\* for \*\*12,000 Gold\*\*

10\. \*\*Equip the Gem XP\*\*



\---

\### 🔹 Powering Up

11\. \*\*2 times\*\* \*Area Lv 850\*

12\. \*\*2 times\*\* \*Area Lv 1000\*

13\. \*\*2 times\*\* \*Area Lv 1200\*

14\. \*\*1 time each\*\* of the following areas:

&#x20;   - Area Lv 1400

&#x20;   - Area Lv 2200

&#x20;   - Area Lv 2666

&#x20;   - Area Lv 3000

&#x20;   - Area Lv 3500

&#x20;   - Area Lv 4000



\---

\### 🔹 Final Boss

15\. Go up to \*Area Lv 75\*, find the \*\*secret path\*\* to the right of the forest (\*Area Lv 2222\*) to be able to defeat the \*\*(third) boss\*\* : \*\*the Acolyte\*\*.

16\. \*\*Put everything into Luck\*\* in the next battles until energy reaches \*\*0\*\* for this run.

&#x20;   - ⚠️ \*\*Tip\*\* : You should have at least \*\*2 energy remaining\*\*, unless you're very unlucky with early low-level enemy fights.

&#x20;   - \*\*Save your last energy\*\* to defeat the boss.



17\. \*\*Defeat the Acolyte\*\* with your stats.

18\. Buy and equip the \*\*Spear\*\* for \*\*32,000 Gold\*\*.



\---

\### 🔹 Final Drop

19\. Stay in \*Area Lv 2222\* and use \*\*all remaining energy\*\* to try and obtain the \*\*accessory drop\*\*.



\---

\### 🔹 Preparation for the Next Run

20\. Before restarting:

&#x20;   - Buy the \*\*Gem Def\*\* for \*\*10,000 Gold\*\* (if budget allows)

&#x20;   - \*\*Re-equip the Gem Hp\*\* once more
