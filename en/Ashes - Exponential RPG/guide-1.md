## 🎯 Objective

**1st Run** : You can defeat the first boss.

---

## 📋 Steps to Follow

### 🔹 Phase 1: Early Game
1. **3 times** *Area Lv 1* with **Half Power** and **All HP** in each battle.
2. **5 times** *Area Lv 25* with **Half Power**, **Half Def**, and **All HP** in each battle.

### 🔹 Phase 2: Initial Equipment
3. Buy and equip the second weapon, named **Knife**, for **2000 Gold**.

### 🔹 Phase 3: Powering Up
4. **1 time** *Area Lv 75* with the same stats (*Half Power, Half Def, All HP*).
5. **5 times** *Area Lv 100* with the same stats.
6. **2 times** *Area Lv 222*.

### 🔹 Phase 4: Gem Xp
7. **1 time** *Area Lv 350*
   → Buy and equip the **Gem Xp** for **5000 Gold**.
8. **3 times** *Area Lv 350*.
9. **4 times** *Area Lv 500*.

### 🔹 Phase 5: Protection Armor
10. Buy and equip the second armor, named **Protection**, for **5000 Gold**.

### 🔹 Phase 6: Boss & Finalization
11. **3 times** *Area Lv 750*
    - You can now defeat the boss at this level.
    - **⚠️ Important** : You should have **5 energy** remaining after defeating the boss.

12. Return to *Area Lv 222* and use all remaining energy to obtain its armor drop.
    - You can put everything into **Luck** here.

13. Before restarting the game:
    → Buy and equip the **Gem Hp** for **10000 Gold**.
