## 🎯 Guide Objectives

### 🔹 **Guide #1**
- **Objective** : Defeat the **first boss** and establish the foundations for subsequent runs.
- **Key Points** :
  - Optimizing statistics (Half Power, Half Def, All HP).
  - Acquiring basic equipment (weapon and armor).
  - Preparing for advanced runs.

### 🔹 **Guide #2**
- **Objective** : Defeat the **(third) boss** and optimize your equipment for more powerful runs.
- **Key Points** :
  - Powering up with high-level zones.
  - Acquiring advanced equipment (Axe, Spear, Gem Def, etc.).
  - Optimizing statistics (All Luck) to maximize drops.
